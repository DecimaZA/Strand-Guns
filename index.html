About Us

Strand Guns is a small outfit with emphasis on personal service. We strive to be competitive while selling quality equipment tailored to the individuals need and budget.

Founded in 1996 by Mossie Mostert, a dear friend and mentor of the current owner, Strand Guns has been operating in the Helderberg basin since 1996.

With over 20 years experience in the industry, we advise our clients to the best of our ability.
